The press release from Citigroup provides an update on the company's financial performance for the fourth quarter of 2024. Here are some key highlights: **Financial Highlights** * Net income: $8.1 billion, up 14% from Q4 2023 * Revenue: $24.6 billion, down 7% from Q4 2023 * Cost of credit: $134 million, compared to $209 million in Q4 2023 **Segment Performance** * **Services**: Net income of $1.9 billion, up 138%, driven by higher revenues and lower expenses. * **Markets**: Net income of $1.0 billion, up from a net loss of $(140) million in Q4 2023, driven by higher revenues and lower expenses. * **Banking**: Net income of $356 million, up 220%, driven by higher revenues and lower expenses. * **Wealth**: Net income of $392 million, up 20%, driven by higher revenues. **Segment Performance Breakdown** * **Services**: Revenue grew 15% to $8.5 billion, driven by higher investment fee revenues and net interest income. * **Markets**: Revenue grew 36% to $4.6 billion, driven by growth in Fixed Income and Equity markets revenues. * **Banking**: Revenue grew 27% to $1.2 billion, driven by growth in Investment Banking fees. * **Wealth**: Revenue grew 20% to $2.0 billion, driven by higher investment fee revenues and net interest income. **Geographic Performance** * Revenue grew 7% globally, driven by growth in the Americas region. **Guidance** * Full-year guidance remains unchanged: Net income expected to be $8.5 billion, revenue expected to be $93.2 billion. Overall, Citigroup reported strong financial performance across most segments, with net income and revenue growth driven by higher revenues and lower expenses. The company's guidance for the full year remains unchanged, indicating confidence in its ability to deliver results.
